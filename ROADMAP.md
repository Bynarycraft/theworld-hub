# Heritage (AEVON) - Expansion Roadmap

## Vision
Build a comprehensive 3D cultural exploration game that showcases traditions, ceremonies, and artifacts from diverse civilizations across all continents.

---

- Core interaction systems

## Planned Phases

### Phase 6: Morocco & North Africa (Berber Culture)
**Region**: Morocco
**Tribe**: Amazigh (Berber)

**Environment**:
- Desert oasis village
- Kasbah fortress with courtyards
- Traditional riad architecture
- Marketplace bazaar

**Missions**:
1. **Carpet Weaving**: Collect colored wool threads
2. **Henna Art**: Find natural pigments for traditional designs
3. **Tea Ceremony**: Gather mint leaves and tea ingredients
4. **Tagine Cooking**: Collect spices and vegetables

**Cultural Elements**:
- Berber symbols and geometric patterns
- Traditional music (oud, bendir drum)
- Marketplace haggling mini-game
- Henna pattern matching puzzle

### Phase 7: South Africa (Zulu & Xhosa)
**Region**: South Africa
**Tribes**: Zulu, Xhosa

**Environment**:
- Savanna grasslands village
- Traditional beehive huts (umuzi)
- Cattle kraal
- Sacred mountain backdrop

**Missions (Zulu)**:

2. **Spear Training**: Ceremonial warrior preparation
3. **Cattle Herding**: Guide livestock to kraal

**Missions (Xhosa)**:
1. **Beadwork Creation**: Traditional color-coded patterns
2. **Initiation Preparation**: Collect ochre for body painting
3. **Stick Fighting**: Training mini-game
4. **Ancestral Offering**: Prepare ritual items

**Cultural Elements**:
- Click language sound integration

## Development Principles

1. **Cultural Authenticity**: Research-based representation with respect
2. **Educational Value**: Teach through interactive experience
3. **Playful Learning**: Missions that feel like games, not lectures
4. **Visual Storytelling**: Minimal text, maximum environmental narrative
5. **Modular Design**: Each region self-contained for easy expansion
6. **Accessibility**: Ensure diverse audiences can engage
7. **Performance**: Maintain 60fps across all regions
8. **Scalability**: Architecture supports infinite content addition

---

## Contribution Guidelines

When adding new regions:
1. Research cultural customs and permissions
2. Consult community representatives when possible
3. Avoid stereotypes and oversimplification
4. Include diverse sub-cultures within regions
5. Balance gameplay with educational goals
6. Test with cultural consultants
7. Document sources and inspirations
